Research Method

For this investigation, my research method is secondary, secondary research consist of already existing data that has already been conducted. I decided to go with secondary research for many reasons which include, firstly, all existing research is being updated regularly do to all the scientific and technology developments every day so the information would be relative.

Advantages

Secondly, I’m not an expert in the field and I was limited on time so going out to do primary research for myself would result in bad and misinformed results and would be null and counterproductive.

My Thinking

I also, focused on properly updated website articles due to the subject of choices as its adapting every day – anything else in the secondary research would be outdated. Therefore, going with secondary research was the correct choice and primary would have been the wrong one for this topic.
