Area 51

This page, Area 51, contains the arguments that prove and disprove the Area 51 conspiracies and involvement with extra-terrestrial life. and I will dive into the most common and theorised conspiracy theories to back up their beliefs.

Fore

In the 1950’s, United States planes were conduction experiments with low-flying aircrafts but there was a constant fear of them being spotted and shot down. To solve this issue, President Eisenhower authorised the development and creation of a top secret, high altitude-recon aircraft called “Project Aquatone”. Aquatone needed to be in a remote, secure location that wasn’t easily accessible to civilians or spies, which led to it being built in Area 51 but the government didn’t even acknowledge its existence until 2013 with the release of declassified CIA reports.

Against

Only 5 years after the development of project Aquatone, 1955, sightings of UFOs were spotted and reported around Area 51. There was a reason for this as Air Force had just started their testing of the newly created U-“ aircraft which could reach highest of more than 60,000 feet which was more than 20,000 feet more than normal military aircraft and 40,000 feet more than commercial airplanes. These tests led to a rise of UFO sightseeing’s, but the Air Force officials knew these weren’t UFOs but instead U-2 testing’s, but they couldn’t tell the public. The government brushed it of by telling the masses with “natural phenomena” and “high-altitude water research”.
